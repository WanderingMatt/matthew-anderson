Onehub

I joined Onehub as the original employee and founding product designer. Here are a few of the things I am most proud of.

Onehub Files

  • Product Manager
  • Principal Designer
  • Front-End Developer

Files are the most critical feature in Onehub and one of my primary goals was to showcase the power of Onehub's simple, role-based permission system. I handled this requirement in two different locations — toolbar buttons and item actions.

The end result is an interface that adapts to changes in permission levels while maintaining a consistent experience for every customer. Permissions can be changed all the way down at an individual item level without page elements moving around or disappearing. You can quickly gauge your current persmissions and determine which folder/files have been interacted with.

Onehub for iOS

  • Product Manager
  • Principal Designer

I designed the first three major releases of Onehub for iOS. This provided me the opportunity to evolve a mobile app through multiple feature launches and across multiple versions of iOS. I managed a remote team of iOS developers for each version.

The end result is a mobile app utilized by half of Onehub customers and supporting every current iOS device. It leverages native iOS libraries as much as possible while supporting all of Onehub's key features.

Company Reviews

  • Leader

I spearheaded and implemented company reviews. After speaking with the entire team, it was clear everyone wanted to have reviews which were:

  • Convenient
  • Insightful
  • Inclusive

After brainstorming and researching dozens of modern implementations, I settled on a modified version of the Stop-Start-Continue process, popularized by Netflix. I combined this with self-selected peer reviews and 360° feedback.

The end result is a company review policy that is still in use today and enjoyed by employees. The review forms facilitate timely but thoughtful reviews and the 360° feedback catalyzed meaningful cultural changes along the way.
