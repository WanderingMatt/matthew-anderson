Hi there. Nice to meet you.

I'm a creative leader and product designer known for my positive attitude, my ability to create and ship products, and for keeping colleagues unstuck.

👋

History

I have 24+ years of experience across multiple disciplines including leadership, design, product management, and front-end development.

Most recently, I was the VP of Product at Onehub where I joined as the first employee. During my 12-year tenure, I helped take Onehub from a rough idea and through multiple iterations — to become a profitable business with a beloved product and a happy team. In a leadership capacity, I was accountable for every aspect of growing a business, creating and leading effective teams, and cultivating a meaningful culture. In a design capacity, I was responsible for every aspect of designing, building, and maintaining multiple, large-scale products.

Prior to that, I worked at a startup called Likewise and ran a freelance design company for a decade.

I co-host a podcast about video games called Chips & Bits.

Style

As a leader, I'm an affiliative coach who always starts with a vision. My leadership style has been described as a "unique brand of radical candor". I strive to enable with trust and guide with inspiration. I foster empathy and transparency amongst my teams. I am always mentoring.

As a designer, I'm a pragmatic craftsman who loves to ship. I'm a full stack designer who is constantly learning and aim to always have a best practice up my sleeve. I advocate for web standards and accessibility. I obsess over tiny details. However, if I have ever achieved anything even remotely close to perfection, it was through seeing my creations in the real world and refining them over time.

Interests

I'm particularly intrigued by multi-faceted roles and open to working in leadership and/or contributor capacities. I would enjoy proving that there are different, better ways of working by joining a company that is trying something out of the ordinary. I prioritize opportunities to work on things that uplift and/or empower people. I'm passionate about design systems, emerging technologies, team building, company culture, and mentorship. I care deeply about video games, music, politics, healthcare, education, and hockey.

Ideally, I'm working on a magical combination of the above.
