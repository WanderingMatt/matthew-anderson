Company Review Policy

  • Design
  • Product Management

I was the creator and implementer of the company review policy. After speaking with the team and researching dozens of modern implementations, I settled on a modified version of the Stop-Start-Continue process, popularized by Netflix. I combined this method with self-selected peer reviews and 360° feedback to create a timely but insightful process.

We started with 4 month cycles and eventually changed to bi-annual reviews.
