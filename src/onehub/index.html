Onehub

I joined Onehub as the original employee and founding product designer. Here are a few of the things I am most proud of.

Onehub.com

  • Product Manager
  • Principal Designer
  • Front-End Developer

I built the first six versions of the Onehub marketing site. The website was constructed mobile-first. Onehub has a completely self-service sign up process; therefore, the site plays a key role in both discovery and sales. Any changes to the site that modify sign up paths are governed by A/B testing. The site is also tightly coupled to search engine marketing efforts. The sign up process can be modified, depending on which marketing campaign brought you to the website.

The end result is a website that has served as Onehub's first point of contact with customers for over a decade. It maintains a high-performing sign up funnel and is the foundation of a profitable business.

Onehub Files

  • Product Manager
  • Principal Designer
  • Front-End Developer

Files are the most critical feature in Onehub and one of my primary goals was to showcase the power of Onehub's role-based permission system. I handled this requirement with expanding and collapsing interface elements at two different locations — toolbar buttons and item actions.

The end result is an interface that adapts to changes in permission levels while maintaining a consistent experience for every customer. Permissions can be changed all the way down at an individual item level without page elements moving around or disappearing. You can quickly gauge your current permissions and determine which folder/files have been interacted with.

Onehub for iOS

  • Product Manager
  • Principal Designer

I designed the first three major releases of Onehub for iOS. This provided me the opportunity to evolve a mobile app through multiple feature launches and across multiple versions of iOS. I managed a remote team of iOS developers for each version.

The end result is a mobile app utilized by half of Onehub customers and supporting every current iOS device. It leverages native iOS libraries as much as possible while supporting all of Onehub's key features.

Onehub Two-Factor Authentication

  • Product Manager
  • Creative Director
  • Front-End Developer

Security and privacy are pillars of Onehub's product strategy. When the time came to add two-factor authentication to the product, I took extra care to make sure that we added the highest level of security without compromising the ease-of-use that Onehub customer's had come to expect. To achieve this I directed the creation of a simple 2FA implementation, working backwards from the enrollee experience.

The end result is 2FA solution that is easy to enroll in, requires no backup codes, and can be optionally enforced at the account level with graceful sign up windows. Powered by a custom TOTP server, codes can be distributed via multiple services depending on availability.

Company Reviews

  • Leader

I spearheaded and implemented company reviews. After interviewing the entire team, it was clear everyone wanted to have reviews which were:

  • Convenient
  • Insightful
  • Inclusive

After brainstorming and researching dozens of modern implementations, I settled on a modified version of the Stop-Start-Continue process, popularized by Netflix. I combined this with self-selected peer reviews and 360° feedback.

The end result is a company review policy that is still in use today and enjoyed by employees. The review forms facilitate timely but thoughtful reviews and the 360° feedback catalyzed meaningful cultural changes along the way.
