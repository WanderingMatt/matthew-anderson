R8R

R8R lets you keep track of your opinions on anything. Create collections and store how you feel with a simple 5-star rating system.

In 2014, I decided to take a simple idea and learn how to develop an iOS app. My goal was to familiarize myself with exactly what was possible when developing an iOS app in order to further inform my designs.

Logo / Icon

  • Principal Designer

I needed the logo to be useful in multiple scenarios including websites, launch images, and app icons. I created a grid of star icons from the app and paired it with a gradient.

The result is an app icon that is recognizable at small sizes and says a lot with a little. The increasing grid of stars conveys the purpose of the app. The added gradient instills a feeling of movement from top to bottom.

iOS App

  • Principal Designer
  • Developer

I was tired of keeping my movie ratings in Netflix, my music ratings in iTunes, and my beer ratings in Untappd. I wanted an app that allowed me to keep all of my ratings in one place and make rating new things as fast as possible.

The result is an app that lets you create collections for anything that you're opinionated about. The app state remembers the last collection you were in so that, in most situations, adding a new rating takes just four taps and a name.
